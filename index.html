We Are educAI

educAI is a non-profit organization geared towards teaching the general population the fundamentals of artificial intelligence through diverse, project-based learning techniques. Engagement and accessibility is our priority. Too many courses emphasize the mathematical jargon involved in the field. We instead offer a more intuitive approach to problem solving in the artificial intelligence domain.

Syllabus

Courses typically last around 8 weeks each, with around two 2hr classes per week. The end goal of this course is for you to be able to implement your own AI models for a specific task (incorporated in your final project). Hopefully, you leave class stimulated to continue learning more and impacting the world with innovation! Here is the rough syllabus (subject to change based on time constraints and other factors).

  • [Week 1] Basic programming review + Introduction to the machine learning frameworks we will be using (sklearn, matplotlib, numpy and pytorch).
  • [Week 2] Introduction to basic machine learning methods (linear regression, k-nearest neighbors, SVM, softmax classification)
  • [Week 3] Introduction to neural networks (MLP, vanilla ANN, batchnorm, dropout, language classification project)
  • [Week 4] Neural networks for images (CNN, image classification project)
  • [Week 5] Neural networks for text (RNN, LSTM, GRU, text generation project)
  • [Week 6] More advanced neural networks (object detection, segmentation, generative methods - VAE's, GAN's)
  • [Week 7] AI in games (Policy gradients, Deep Q learning, actor-critic methods, start your own project based on what you've learned so far (!!!))
  • [Week 8] Work time! (Work on your projects, present on final day)
